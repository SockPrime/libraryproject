Activities

Whats fun around here

Float by the flamingos: The best time to see them is during November to April, You should also start your tour at 9am. There are two places you can go to float with them, Celestun or Rio Lagartos.

Swim with whale sharks: the best places to swim with sharks is in La Paz in the sea, the best time to visit is during October to April with the highest chance of sightings is during December and February.

The day of the dead: The day of the dead is a cultural celebration held on November 1st and 2nd, to honor and pay respect to your dead loved ones. Families create ofrendas which they put food, drinks, candles, and personal items to welcome spirits.
